OUR
VISION

We had an idea.

An idea to make a change in this world.
We aspired to somehow merge Pragati and Raksha together to make this world a better place, starting with our very own country. So that is the story of how “PRAGSHA” was born.

We work towards an ideal desi utopian vision for every single person from all cultures and ethnicities to live in harmony.

We ensure that every part of untouched India grows as a whole to make the entire country have its biggest glow up and to become the greatest of all time in every single aspect of world competition in providing premium disaster management software for both natural and societal calamities which include earthquakes, fires, as well as lack of emergency healthcare and resources for all, alike.

Kyuki agar ek baar jud gaya India, tabhi aage badhega India.

Because at the end of the day our ultimate vision is to revitalize India, striving to match and even surpass the greatness it once embodied, owing to its abundant wealth and treasured heritage. Within the intricate tapestry of its walls and roads lie countless untold stories, waiting to be discovered, and we aspire to be a small part of those stories.
