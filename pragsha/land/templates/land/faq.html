FAQs

Social
1 What is Pragsha?

Pragsha is an advanced central database application designed to enhance the coordination and efficiency of emergency response efforts. It allows rescue agencies to register critical information such as their location, area of expertise, and more, while also offering a user-friendly interface for citizens to locate nearby rescue services during emergencies.

2 Can a person trust Pragsha with its personal details?

We are using password hashing for this purpose to make sure that every citizen's personal detail is secured in here.

3 How can one make sure the agencies are not fake?

Our web application provides firm registration and verification process for agencies to make sure the viability of that agency.

4 Do we get immediate help on emergency?

Yes, Pragsha provides you with a unique emergency SOS button for immediate needs. This includes direct interaction with the agencies.

5 If we inquire in agency forum, what will be the minimum time they will revert back?

Usually agencies take 3 to 5 business days to answer non-immediate inquiries. Meanwhile, users can inquire in the user-to-user forum.

6 What if my network is not working properly during an emergency?

Pragsha has an exclusive progressive web application which provides offline functionality and accessibility.

7 If I broadcast an emergency what is the response time like?

Pragsha gives you an inbuilt map to locate the progress of the rescue operation that is coming to help you.

8 How to get help with the technical issues?

Pragsha gives you an email contact for any technical issues faced by the citizen which is accessible by our team. A response will be given within 24 hours.

9 Why should one choose Pragsha?

Pragsha is a perfect platform for those who are in need. Many people are not aware of the small agencies that are available nearby who work efficiently to save people.
